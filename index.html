Founded in 2021, Red Thorn Interactive hopes to elevate Bangladesh’s game development scene to the next level. We hope to offer Bangladesh’s talented game artists and passionate video game developers a chance to showcase their innovativeness and their love for the art form on an international stage.
We aim to innovate with every project while drawing inspiration from the titles and series that have shined brightest throughout the history of the video game industry.
We thank you for your support and kind wishes.
