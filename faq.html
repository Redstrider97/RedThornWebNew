GAMEPLAY


Q. How many units will there be the choose from?

A. Over 100 at Early Access.

Q. Can the player control any character other than AlexanderBall?

A. The player can choose other commanders from amongst Alexander’s Companions.

Q. In a sentence, how would you describe the combat system?

A. Heavily inspired by the likes of Heroes of Might and Magic.

Q. In a sentence, how would you describe the overworld gameplay?

A. A mix of Wargroove and Paradox Interactive titles.

Q. How does this game offer replayability?

A. Hopefully, the astronomical combination of armies possible through this game will encourage players to try out specific army builds to see if they can beat that game with a new army build each time. We are hoping the multiplayer mode will encourage groups of players to try out new army builds against one another.

THE HISTORY

Q. Why the story of Alexander the Great?

A. The diversity in his army can be very well represented through the colorful Countryball art style and allows room for an interesting reexamination of the short-lived Macedonian Empire.

Q. Is this game historically accurate?

A. We are trying our best. The dizzying volume of individuals that are named in the historical sources depicting this time period are not captured in this game because we believe it simply cannot be fully captured within a video game, especially not a video game by a studio of our size. Nonetheless, extensive historical research has gone into the game. All decisions are a product of the balancing act between fun game design and historical accuracy.

Q. What time period is the game based in?

A. The game starts at 336 BC.

Q. How many campaigns will there be at Early Access?

A. One.

Q. How long will a single run-through of a campaign typically take?

A. We are aiming for 10-15 hours.
